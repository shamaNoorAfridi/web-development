COLOR PALLETE

Primary Colors Pallete

Primary colors are the basic building blocks of all other colors. They cannot be created by mixing other colors together.

There are three primary colors:

    Red

    Blue

    Yellow

Secondary Colors Pallete

Secondary colors are created by mixing two primary colors together.

There are three secondary colors:

    Orange

    (made by mixing red and yellow)

    Green

    (made by mixing blue and yellow)

    Purple

    (made by mixing blue and red)

Tertiary Colors Pallete

Tertiary colors are created by mixing a primary color with a secondary color.

There are six tertiary colors:

    Red-Orange

    (mix of red and orange)

    Yellow-Orange

    (mix of yellow and orange)

    Yellow-Green

    (mix of yellow and green)

    Blue-Green

    (mix of blue and green)

    Blue-Purple

    (mix of blue and purple)

    Red-Purple

    (mix of red and purple)
